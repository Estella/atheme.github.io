Atheme IRC Services

The Atheme IRC Platform is a IRCv3.1-compliant set of middleware (also known as Services) for IRC networks. (As of late February 2016) Development is starting back up!

It is typically combined with an IRCv3.1 compliant IRC daemon. Find out more about IRCv3 at ircv3.net.

Please pardon us while we update atheme.net and rebuild the Atheme Development Group website.
