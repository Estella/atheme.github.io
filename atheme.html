Atheme IRC Services

Lorum ipsum blah blah blah

Ubiquity

Not only are more and more networks using Atheme every day, but some of IRC's most well-known networks are already using Atheme. Networks such as freenode, EsperNet and DarkMyst are all relying on Atheme IRC services. With networks everywhere turning to Atheme, it's the best choice for an administrator wanting to give their users a familiar interface to the usefulness of IRC services.

Feature-Packed

Atheme has all the features you've come to expect from modern IRC services, such as BotServ and HostServ, as well as innovative new features, such as GroupServ and InfoServ.

Fast + Efficient

The Internet is faster than ever before. Computers are exponentially speedier than any time in the past. Shouldn't your IRC services be fast too? Atheme IRC services not only are lightning fast, but are also the most scalable modern IRC services package available.

Compatability

Atheme supports linking to over twenty different IRC daemons. For a full list of supported IRCds, view our compatability report.

Customisable

Atheme comes prebundled with over 250 modules and offers an additional 52 modules from our contributed modules repository.

Extensible

Even something as featureful as Atheme might not have everything your network needs. For those cases, Atheme offers both an extremely clean C API and a powerful, well-structured Perl interface. Extending services has never been easier!
